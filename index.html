Welcome to Puthuppady: A Serene Escape in Kozhikode District Discover Puthuppady Nestled in the heart of Kerala’s Kozhikode district, Puthuppady is a picturesque village that offers a tranquil retreat from the hustle and bustle of city life. Known for its lush greenery, serene landscapes, and rich cultural heritage, Puthuppady is the perfect destination for those seeking both relaxation and adventure. Puthuppady is renowned for its breathtaking natural beauty. The rolling hills, verdant tea plantations, and serene water bodies paint a perfect picture of rural Kerala. Whether you're taking a leisurely stroll through the verdant fields or enjoying a quiet moment by the lake, the scenic beauty of Puthuppady will captivate your senses. Nestled in the Western Ghats of Kerala, Thamarassery Churam is one of the most picturesque and significant mountain passes in the state. Known for its breathtaking views, lush greenery, and winding roads, Thamarassery Churam offers an unforgettable journey through the scenic landscapes of the Kozhikode district. As you ascend or descend Thamarassery Churam, prepare to be captivated by panoramic vistas of the Western Ghats. The winding road provides awe-inspiring views of verdant hills, deep valleys, and mist-covered peaks. Every turn reveals a new facet of nature’s grandeur, making the journey itself a memorable experience.
